\begin{abstract}
We formalize Binary Neural Networks (BNNs) as discrete path-sum models and connect them to SCRFP. 
We prove equivalence to sparse binary-gated Mixture of Experts and propose experiments and visualizations 
that expose the implicit routing structure in quantized networks.
\end{abstract}
